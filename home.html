About our company

We like nice weather. That's why we left the cold and hopped on a plane (okay, many planes) to the hottest place we could imagine...Singapore. In between sitting on the beach and eating street food, we've been known to do some work, sometimes. We believe great products transcend national and cultural borders. If we like it, we hope you do too!

Our team has gathered from around the world -- and before coming together, we have built and managed companies that have made a difference across many different industries. We are building technology that sits at the intersection of the latests trends in mobile applications, and we passionately believe that it will create value for communities.

We Invent

We Represent
